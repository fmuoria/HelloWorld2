Our Heritage & Values

PEFA Heritage

The Pentecostal Evangelistic Fellowship of Africa (PEFA) has a rich heritage spanning several decades. Founded with a vision to spread the Gospel across Africa, PEFA has grown to become a significant force in African Christianity.

Our church is proud to be part of this movement, carrying forward the traditions of evangelical fervor, Pentecostal spirituality, and community service that define PEFA.

Kabuku Community

PEFA Kabuku Church has been deeply rooted in the local community since its establishment. We have witnessed and participated in the growth and development of the Kabuku area.

Our commitment to the community goes beyond Sunday services - we are actively involved in education, health initiatives, and social welfare programs that benefit our neighbors.

Looking Forward

As we reflect on God's faithfulness in our past, we look forward with anticipation to what He will do in the future. Our vision remains the same: to know Christ and make Him known in our community and beyond.

Our Commitment for the Future

We are committed to continuing the legacy of faithful service, community engagement, and spiritual growth that has marked our church from the beginning. By God's grace, we will continue to be a beacon of hope and transformation in Kabuku and beyond.
